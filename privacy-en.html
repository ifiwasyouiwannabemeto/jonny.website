When visiting the website, technical data (IP address, time, browser type, operating system) is automatically collected and stored in so-called server log files. This data is used exclusively for system security and error analysis and is not evaluated on a personal basis.

Contact Form

When you contact me via the contact form, the following information is transmitted:

  • Name (required)
  • Email address (required)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Type of event (optional)
  • Preferred date (optional)
  • Message (required)

The processing is carried out via the service Formspree (Formspree Inc., USA). Your information will only be used to process your request and will not be used for advertising purposes.
The data will generally be deleted after 6 months at the latest, unless there is a legal obligation to retain it.

Cookies, Scripts & Tracking

This website does not use cookies for advertising, analysis or tracking purposes. Only technically necessary scripts (e.g. for animations or form functions) are used, which do not store any personal data.

JavaScript and CSS libraries are loaded via so-called Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to display and operate the website. These are technically necessary processes that do not collect or store any personal data.

External Links (Social Media)

The website contains links to external platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Spotify). When accessing these links, the respective privacy policies of the third-party providers apply. I have no influence on their data processing.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you generally have the following rights:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to object to processing

If you believe that the processing of your data violates data protection law, you can complain to me or to the Austrian Data Protection Authority at www.dsb.gv.at.
