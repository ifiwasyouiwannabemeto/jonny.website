About Me

I’m DJ Jonny – passionate DJ, music enthusiast, and event professional for over 20 years. I grew up in the Zell am See district of Salzburg, where I discovered my love for music at an early age. What started at small local parties evolved into a full-time career with hundreds of unforgettable gigs.


For me, music is more than just sound – it creates connection, sparks emotion, and defines the moment. My sets range from electronic beats and club anthems to tailor-made playlists. I read the crowd, feel the energy, and use technical expertise to shape the perfect sound and atmosphere.


I collaborate with event organizers, companies, and private clients – performing at events across Austria and beyond. I believe the spotlight shouldn’t be on the DJ, but on the experience of the guests. Every event is unique, and I treat it that way. My goal? To make every party one to remember.
